Championing African creativity — one artist, one story, one stage at a time.

Suchet is a passionate creative force and the visionary founder of Arts and Vibes — Nigeria's thriving platform for art, culture, and community. With an unwavering belief in the power of artistic expression to transform lives and shift narratives, she has spent years building spaces where creatives don't just survive — they flourish.

The Story

A life devoted
to art and its people

Suchet didn't set out to build an empire — she set out to build a home for creatives. Born with an eye for beauty and a heart for community, she recognised early that Northern Nigeria held an ocean of artistic talent that the world had barely glimpsed.

In 2021, that recognition became Arts and Vibes: a movement. A community platform that now reaches over 200,000 social media impressions, has welcomed more than 1,000 attendees across live events, and has collaborated with over 60 artists spanning poetry, visual arts, performance, and beyond.

Suchet leads with intention. Whether she is curating an exhibition, mentoring an emerging poet, or organising a workshop that gives young people their first stage — she brings the same fierce dedication: art deserves to be seen, and artists deserve to be supported.

Her work is a living testament to the idea that creativity is not a luxury. It is a language. And she is determined to make sure that language is spoken fluently, loudly, and proudly across Africa and beyond.

Arts and Vibes

Building the largest community
of creatives in Northern Nigeria

Arts and Vibes is more than a brand — it is a living ecosystem where poets, painters, performers, and storytellers find not just an audience, but each other. Founded on the conviction that art can drive dialogue and dialogue can drive change, it has become the premier platform for cultural expression in the region.

From workshops and residencies to flagship competitions like the Labari Prize for Poetry, Arts and Vibes consistently creates the conditions for artists to make their most daring, most honest, most necessary work.

"We are creating the largest community of creatives looking for an outlet to create, collaborate, and have creative discourse."

Community Events

Carefully curated gatherings — exhibitions, open mics, panel discussions — that turn strangers into collaborators and audiences into movements.

Workshops & Training

Hands-on skill-building programmes for emerging artists and youth, giving them the craft, confidence, and connections they need to thrive.

The Labari Prize for Poetry

An annual literary competition celebrating voices that explore identity, postcolonialism, and what it means to be Northern Nigerian and African today.

Artist Collaboration

A robust network of 60+ artists across disciplines, fostering partnerships that push creative boundaries and amplify African stories.

Visual & Performing Arts

Championing the full spectrum of creative expression — from canvas to stage — with platforms that make Northern Nigerian artistry visible to the world.

Youth Empowerment

Investing in the next generation of African creators — providing mentorship, resources, and stages to those who have the talent but not yet the platform.
