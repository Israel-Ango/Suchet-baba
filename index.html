About

Suchet Baba is a thinker, creative writer, painter and cultural practitioner who advocates for afrocentrism and decolonization of the African mind. She is the founder and convener of Arts and Vibes, a festival she started in Kaduna to create a platform where the artists and youth in northern Nigeria can commune without the ethnographic pressures that lie in the state.

In 2023 she was a Seed Award Recipient from the Prince Claus Fund in partnership with the British Council. She was also shortlisted for the 2023 Alinea Prize in Nonfiction. Her works have been featured in several literary magazines including Brittle Paper, Kalahari Review, Punocracy and others.

Festival

Suchet has been championing a movement for Northern Nigerian art through her platform, Arts and Vibes, which she founded in 2021. Through exhibitions, festivals, and cultural programming, she has created opportunities for Northern Nigerian artists to showcase their work, challenge stereotypes, and contribute to broader conversations about contemporary African art and identity.
